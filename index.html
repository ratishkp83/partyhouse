About this venue

Welcome to Mumbai's most sought-after private party rooftop! Perched on the 18th floor with panoramic views of the Bandra-Worli Sea Link, this venue transforms any occasion into an unforgettable experience.

Whether it's an intimate anniversary dinner for two, a wild birthday bash for 50, or a family reunion — The Skyline delivers. The space includes a fully-equipped bar, a dance floor, LED mood lighting, a sound system, and a dedicated host who ensures everything runs perfectly.
